Mango Crepe Roll

Ingredients:

  • 4 eggs
  • sugar 64g
  • cake flour 130g
  • milk 360g
  • corn oil 30g
  • whipping cream 300g
  • 2 mangos

Steps:

  1. Make Crepe Batter: pour milk, cake flour, corn oil, eggs, 40g sugar into a big bowl, mix them thoroughly, filter the mixed batter to be smooth
  2. Cook Crepes: warm a 8 inch non-stick pan over low heat, pour one soup spoon of batter into the pan, tilt the pan to spread the batter evenly, wait for 3 minutes or until there appears some bubbles on the crepe, use a spatula to peel the edges, transfer the cooked crepe into a cooling plate.
  3. Prepare Mango Pieces: peel mangos, throw away the mango seed, cut the mangos into pieces, put them into a bowl.
  4. Whipping Cream Filling: pour whipping cream and sugar into a big bowl, beat them until stiff peaks form.
  5. Assemble Crepe Rolls: overlap three crepes on a baking map, use a cake spatula to apply whipped cream filling evenly on the crepes, spread mango pieces on the filling evenly, add one more layer of whipped cream filling over the mangos, fold in the edges and roll up tightly in a log.
  6. Chill in the fridge for 1h.
